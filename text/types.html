Abbreviations¶

Multiple anonymous parameters or results may be combined into a single declaration:

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll} \def\mathdef2780#1{{}}\mathdef2780{parameter} & \def\mathdef2847#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2847{(}~~\def\mathdef2848#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2848{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef2849#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2849{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef2850#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2850{(}~~\def\mathdef2851#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2851{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef2852#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2852{)})^\ast \\ \def\mathdef2780#1{{}}\mathdef2780{result} & \def\mathdef2853#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2853{(}~~\def\mathdef2854#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2854{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef2855#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2855{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef2856#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2856{(}~~\def\mathdef2857#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2857{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef2858#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2858{)})^\ast \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]
